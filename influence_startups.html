LYFLINK

To which Sector does it belong?

It belongs to Electronic Health Services which tries to connect doctors across India. They provide consultation through telephone, ordering medicines online, connecting with many doctors via online meetings

How Media helped during covid ?

Adoption of social media to reach the public helped in getting people in different locations, since there has been an increase in usage of social media in the pandemic times

Before COVID

Lyflink was started in 2018, before the pandemic it doesn't have that huge reach which is currently present now.

after covid

Present it is a one of the successful startup , generating a revenue of $5 Million

myupchar

To which Sector does it belong?

myUpchar is a leading online doctor consultation app. Their mission is to provide reliable information on health to be available to everyone in various languages.It is available in more than 13 languages currently

How Media helped during covid ?

They believe that everyone must have access to health information through the internet. They have facebook and instagram pages on which they post videos on health conditions and treatments and promote their bussiness.

Before COVID

Started in 2016 , before the pandemic it doesn't have that huge craze among the 2,3,4 tier people which is currently present now.

after covid

Currently accessed by more than 10 million people and myUpchar raises $5 million from Nexus,Omidyar,etc

Nas academy

To which Sector does it belong?

It is a one stop academy to learn the skills of ighly-engaging courses across content creation, art, business, crypto currency and many more. People are joining from across 140 countries.

How Media helped during covid ?

The founder of Nas Academy is a youtuber , who posts videos only for 1 min which is really valuable and this created a hype and trend through which he advertises his brand and then they use the digital marketing to sell their courses.

Before COVID

It was founded in Feb 1, 2020.Although it started in amid COVID Pandemic they manage to scale it.

after covid

They have managed to rise $11 Million with 14 investors and currently they giving employment to 100-200 people.

markitup

To which Sector does it belong?

Before COVID
