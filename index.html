Do Really Media has Significance in our LIVES?

Yes, really it has a huge influece on societies in many ways. Over past many years there has been a huge development in technology which led to develop the Media Industry a lot and now it has become a part and parcel of our lives.And especially during COVID Pandemic it was the source which made connections/communication among ourselves to connect with each other during the critical times which gives the hope.And during COVID media has influeced a lot many other sectors and had a huge growth in their respective sectors.
